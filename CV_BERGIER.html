Research Profile

My research lies at the intersection of formal logic, philosophy of the formal sciences (logic, mathematics, theoretical computer science), and logic-based artificial intelligence. I work primarily on intensional logic, formal semantics, and formal ontologies, examining their philosophical and epistemological implications for computation and AI. My approach combines theoretical and philosophical analysis of formal systems with applied formal research in program analysis, knowledge representation, and symbolic AI.
